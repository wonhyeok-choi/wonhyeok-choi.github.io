Wonhyeok Choi

Hi, I'm Wonhyeok Choi, a M.S.-Ph.D. integrated course student at the Department of Electrical Engineering & Computer Sciences (EECS) at DGIST, South Korea. I am conducting my research under the supervision of Prof. Sunghoon Im at the DGIST Computer Vision Lab.

Previously, I earned the B.S. degree from the Department of School of Undergraduate Studies, DGIST, in 2022, and I was fortunate to have the opportunity to study as an exchange student at UC Berkeley during my undergraduate years and later visit Stanford as a visiting scholar during my Ph.D. program.

My research interest is computer vision and deep learning, with a particular emphasis on solving Dynamic Neural network, Multi-task/Multi-modal learning & Meta-learning, and 3D perception tasks.
